House blend coffee


From our family's house to yours. This delicious well-rounded coffee is roasted medium-dark with a rich flavor and a smooth, clean finish.


Caratheristics

Before coffee is roasted, the green coffee beans smell like fresh vegetables and sweet grains. Coffee roasting is a heating process that turns these green coffee beans into the fragrant, dark brown beans that we enjoy each morning. It’s through roasting that a coffee’s unique aroma, acidity, body—and, of course, flavor—are cultivated.
